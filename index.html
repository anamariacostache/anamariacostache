Anamaria Costache

Hi there! I work as a Research Scientist at Intel AI Research in San Diego. I completed my PhD at Bristol University, under the supervision of Nigel Smart. I am interested in privacy-preserving machine learning, fully homomorphic encryption and more broadly, computing on encrypted data, lattice-based and post-quantum cryptography. You can contact me at anamaria.costache1992@gmail.com .

I completed my undergraduate and Masters degree at Warwick University, where I studied number theory with a focus on algebraic number theory. My final year dissertation was on Brauer Groups. See here for a full CV.

Away from work, I enjoy bouldering, reading and travelling.
