Anamaria Costache

Hi there! I am a PhD student at Bristol University, working with Nigel Smart. I am interested in fully homomorphic encryption and more broadly, computing on encrypted data, lattice-based and post-quantum cryptography. My email is anamaria.costache@bristol.ac.uk.

I completed my undergraduate and Masters degree at Warwick University, where I studied number theory with a focus on algebraic number theory. My final year dissertation was on Brauer Groups.

Away from work, I enjoy bouldering, reading and travelling.
