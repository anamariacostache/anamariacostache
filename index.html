Anamaria Costache

Hi there! I am currently a PhD student at Bristol University, under the supervision of Nigel Smart. Previously, I was at Warwick University, from where I got an MMath. My email is anamaria.costache@bristol.ac.uk.

I am interested in fully homomorphic encryption and more broadly, computing on encrypted data, lattice-based and post-quantum cryptography. Back at Warwick, I studied number theory with a focus on algebraic number theory. My final year dissertation was on Brauer Groups.

In my free time, I enjoy bouldering, reading and travelling. The above picture was taken in Vinales, Cuba.
